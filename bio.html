Olá!

Born in 86 in the second largest capital of Latin America, São Paulo, I moved to Bahia at the age of 7, where I encountered one of my great passions, the sea.

As an adult, I moved to Florianópolis to pursue another great love: anthropology.
I spent some of the best years of my life there.

A few years later, I moved to Germany, where, for almost 10 years now, “makes the familiar strange and the strange familiar” has become a daily task.

Books, cuisine, music, dance and relationships of solidarity make my life even more interesting.
